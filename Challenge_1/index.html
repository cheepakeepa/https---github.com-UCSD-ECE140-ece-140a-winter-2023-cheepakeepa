Product Thinking Vocabulary

  • Pain Killer

    • Refers to products of a type that are absolutely needed

  • Vitamin

    • Refers to products that aren't necessarily needed but are nice to have, for example designer clothes. Something "Nice to have"

  • Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

    • The most barebones of a product prototype that can be used to test whether the product idea works / is viable
